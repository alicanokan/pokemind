How to play

1 · Open on your phone 📱 Scan the Play QR (or tap Play AR). Allow camera, location and motion — that's how Pokémon appear in your world.
2 · Hunt & catch 🎯 The map shows where Pokémon hide. Walk within 40 m, look around through the camera, and tap them to catch!
3 · Claim your space 🚩 Walk around your home (or trace it on the map) — it becomes YOUR territory with your name on it.
4 · Decorate & share 🎨 Place Pokémon in your space as AR artworks and print your home QR — friends scan it and see your collection floating in your rooms.

Indoors, GPS wobbles — open the map and tap 🎯 I'm here on your exact spot for table-level precision. On a local dev server, if the browser warns about the certificate tap Show details → Visit website.
